1. Cabins in the USA

Airbnb name: Dunlap Hollow A-Frame

This about the Entire cabin in Rockbridge, Ohio, United States.
Away from the crowded cities and towns. You can find peace here.
I had been dreaming of living in the woods and taking in the lap of pure beauty that is Hills for a while when the perfect opportunity presented itself. I was invited to stay at a new cabin called Dunlap Hollow for a night and it was honestly so amazing and I am here to share the experience.

The Dunlap Hollow A-Frame is a custom luxury A-Frame completed in 2021. The A-Frame sleeps up to 10 guests with 3 bedrooms and a picturesque loft filled with windows that sleeps 4. Here is a list of the beds in each room:

  • Bedroom 1: King
  • Bedroom 2: Queen
  • Bedroom 3: Queen with private balcony
This also has full service kitchen where I cooked myself and you can too if you want. This cabin has 4.95 rating on Airbnb.

2. House next to a beach

Airbnb name: Straumen Sea View - Magic Arctic Getaway

I have spent entire week in this very special cabin located right at the seafront in Norway.
You can always see the beach and sea right from you window. And the beautiful sunset.
The cabin is fully equipped with everything you will need and the bathroom is spacious with water closet and large shower. Washing machine/tumbling dryer and dishwasher is also available and can be used freely.

If you are looking for peace, seclusion, tranquility, nature and a great place to stay in the Arctic, then look no further, because you've just found it. It has following specifications:

  • Bedroom 1: 1 double bed
